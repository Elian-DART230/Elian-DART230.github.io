Life and its whims

Time?perhaps it is only an illusion that is spoken of

Time that takes life and with it our loved ones

It is so absurd that I swim in the abyss of meaninglessness

Where nihilism is my father

Father that only shows me the truth

The truth that is nothing more than a cruel vision of reality

Life is nothing more than a river that I am angry about, of its inserted and unjust whims

Poverty, death and greed, things that I can only complain about because they are all over the world

Things that a simple optimistic thought would be seen as something mediocre.

But, there is only mediocre left when those who have power,

only look out for themselves and for themselves.

Happy knowing that those with power,

are also victims of what we all see wrong in society and with that, I submerge myself in a river without causes known as life.
